<decisions_made>

- Flexible DAC bit depth (8/12/16) over fixed 8-bit — gives tonal range from authentic to clean
- Switchable stepped/interpolated scanning — authenticity vs modern smoothness
- Both polyphonic and unison voice modes
- Per-voice/global switchable ASR envelope
- Support both single-cycle and multi-frame WAV formats
- Rack 2 primary target, Rack 3 compatible (avoid deprecated APIs)
- C++11 standard (hardcoded in SDK compile.mk)
- dr_wav for WAV loading (same as VCV Fundamental)
- SIMD float_4 for polyphonic processing (two groups of 4 = 8 voices)
- Existing sibling plugin at ../vco/ has working Makefile pattern to copy
- Mip-mapped band-limited tables are mandatory from Phase 1 (cannot be retrofitted)
</decisions_made>
